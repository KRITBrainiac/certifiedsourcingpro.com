About Us

SourcePro, in a nutshell, is a three-day long brief but inclusive course aimed at upgrading skills of sourcing agents in finding the perfect professionals. It is accordingly divided into three sections â€“ SourcePro Passive Search 1, SourcePro Passive Search 2 and SourcePro Telephone Sourcing.

The program trains attendees on refining and fine tuning their searches using both the basic and the advanced. In the first day, for instance, they are taught how to do the basics like comprehending the job description, using alternative keywords as tools to find candidates as per job description, look for target companies, do Boolean searches, availing Google by setting search strings using different keywords and profile screening.

The next day, use of different social networking sources like Twitter, Linkedin, Facebook, Jigsaw and Bloomberg, advanced techniques in Boolean search, various other methods of setting search strings is taught in depth.

The course targets new avenues for procuring suitable candidates using these methods much more innovatively instead of the traditional job portals. Besides the mandatory, the course also reminds attendees about the basic in any job search like a thorough and proper requirement of the job and researching on it by using specific keywords.

It also gives training on telephone sourcing. Its overall goal is to teach agents new tricks and methods to source hitherto unnoticed, inconspicuous passive candidates.
