Q1. What are the certification courses SourcePro offers?

A: BasicPRO The session aims at giving attendees a thorough grasp of Boolean Search and also a deep in-sight into Top Indian Job Portals that would help build a solid substructure which in turn would increase efficiency and productivity.

SocialPRO – This session aims to keep you updated of the latest trends and the basics of how to use social media for talent sourcing.This would help recruiters unlock new doors and explore new avenues beyond traditional sourcing techniques.

ResearchPRO - We have put together a great learning module to let you learn uncovering a vast world of search engines and advance tools to find those “hidden nuggets” your competitors are missing out.

HeadhuntPRO- The course trains on Headhunting Techniques to scout for passive candidates who do not register themselves on popular Job Portals and either shy away from Social Media or restrict their presence on the Internet. This course helps to figure out different ways to discover the “Off-the-radar” talent in the market.



Q2. What results can I expect?

A: You learn the best practices to find talent using advanced tools, Google, Social Media etc. This will help individuals to increase their productivity at work by finding those hard-to-find candidates. You will realize that your productivity, number of candidates and quality is going up.



Q3. Do I receive any study material?

A: Yes. You will receive a training presentation after the Webinar.



Q4. What is the time duration for each module?

A: Each module webinar will require 3 - 4 hours.



Q5. How much time will I need to dedicate to study?

A: We understand Recruiters are very busy with their everyday tasks; however we expect you to spend 20-30 minutes at least once a week may be over the weekend. The more time you invest in practicing the better you’ll get at it.



Q6. How is this certification different from others?

A: SourcePro Certification is designed for Indian Talent Acquisition professionals, by Indian Talent Acquisition Professionals.The course material has been designed and put together by industry experts who have years of experience in the sourcing domain, have thorough understanding of global practices and are very well networked.The certification is a result of tried and tested sourcing methods and we have successful stories from our clients.
