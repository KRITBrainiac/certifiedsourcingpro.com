About Certification

The talent acquisition scenario is witnessing a transformation owing to acute talent-wars and changing dynamics of digital channels. A resourceful talent professional is one who stays thoroughly abreast of all the latest changes and constantly improvises those to mine suitable candidates. Our goal is to help bring the benefits of change to Indian talent acquisition professionals through excellence, innovation and recognition in new age talent sourcing techniques.

Problem as Opportunity


It’s a fact that most of the recruiting professionals start their recruitment career not by design! They either learn HRM before turning to recruiting or land this industry by accident.

  • No Formal Training is available to new recruiting professionals before starting their Careers. We strongly believe that recruiting has moved beyond than “Post and Pray” approach where recruiters need a learning support to improve efficiency.
  • Change is the name of the game. Old techniques of talent search may not be equally effective considering the digital / talent transformation we have witnessed in past few years. Recruiters need to learn and adapt this change to be in the game.
  • Thankless Job. Being in the support function not necessarily means staying away from recognition and fame. Best of the breed recruiters deserve to stand out of the crowd and be superstars.

Rather than looking at these as problems, we see them as opportunity to engage, collaborate and learn from each other. SourcePro, a long brief but inclusive certification course, provides sourcing professionals tools to become resourceful enough to keep abreast of the all the developments and innovate continuously to mine candidates.
