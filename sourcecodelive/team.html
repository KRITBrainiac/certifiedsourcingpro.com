Our Team

Yusuf Pathan -

Chief Lead Trainer




Managing Director of Key Resourcing, Yusuf Pathan is considered one of the most original and creative thought leaders of the talent acquisition domain. As a standard bearer, Yusuf takes care of the strategy and operations of Key Resourcing to make sure the company’s growth graph steadily shoots up.

He started off as a Data Entry Operator way back in 1998 at a very young age and gradually accumulated a wealth of experience in the sourcing industry through sheer hardwork and focus. In his 16 years of professional life, 14 years of which in RPO, He assumed key managerial roles rather early and handled those with dexterity. He has partnered with many of the world’s leading executive search and recruiting firms like Highland Partners, Hudson, Boydens, Talent2, etc. to support their executive search research and sourcing teams with highly targeted ‘name generation’, 'name sourcing' ‘long list generation’ and ‘passive candidate sourcing’ solutions. Yusuf is also a level two Certified People Sourcer.

Key Resourcing founded by him in 2009 with a handful of employees, is a rapidly expanding RPO with globally interlinked, adept and qualified professionals.

Having an affable personality, Yusuf is also a skilled orator. In his free time, he likes to travel and do landscape and portrait photography.

Chinmay Chavan -
Lead Trainer



Currently a Lead Trainer, Chinmay has been in the sourcing domain for last five years. He has deep knowledge and practical experience in Internet Research, Name Sourcing, CV Sourcing, Candidate Screening, Job Portal Screening, Executive Search, Talent Acquisition and Recruiting. He began his career as a Customer Service Associate at Wipro BPO, but soon enough moved to sourcing by joining OS2i as a Name Generation Executive. Chinmay quickly proved his mettle to bag crucial roles in OS2i, including that of Research Associate and Business Development. He joined Key Resourcing as a Research Consultant and was promoted to the next level of Senior Research Consultant pretty fast. He likes to hike or click pictures in his free time. “Recruitment to me is not just a career, it’s become an essential part of life,” says Chinmay.

Jasmine Joseph -

Head of Course Administration




Resourceful, young and dynamic, Jasmine is currently the Head of Course Administration at sourcePro. She has been working with Key Resourcing for 4.5 years. She has gathered a strong understanding of full cycle recruitment, including internet research, name sourcing and CV sourcing. Besides, she has knowledge and experience in Business Development, Resource Management, Client Relationship Development, Team Building and Operational Delivery. Jasmine began as Talent Acquisition Executive at Key Resourcing and prior to that she had a stint at Wipro MNC where she held various crucial roles like Senior Associate and Voice Coach for the United Airlines. A movie buff, Jasmine likes to unwind watching good English and Hindi movies. She also loves reading in her free time.
