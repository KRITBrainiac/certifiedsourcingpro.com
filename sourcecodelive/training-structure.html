HeadhuntPRO

HeadhuntPRO is a specially designed to enable talent professionals source passive candidates directly from competitors or companies within a similar industry. Instead of the usual “you find us” method used by majority of recruiters, “Name Generation” or “Headhunting” helps in identifying the untapped pool of candidates using the “we’ll find you” method.


The course teaches Headhunting Techniques to scout for inconspicuous candidates who do not register themselves on popular Job Portals and either shy away from Social Media or restrict their presence on the Internet. This course helps to figure out different ways to discover the “off-the-radar” talent in the market. Techniques learnt in this training session are very beneficial for gathering information on organization structure in a company, team mapping, etc.

  • Introduction to Telephone Name Sourcing

  • Candidate or Lead Generation through Telephone

  • Organisation Research

    • Understanding the team structure

    • Getting information on the right people

  • Tools used for Telephone Sourcing

  • Tips and Tricks for Telephone Sourcing
